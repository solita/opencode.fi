Background

Brief background

Publicsource.fi -service provides a list of Finnish public sector IT projects, which have published their source code in the internet. The web site is maintained by Solita ltd. and the information can be utilized by third parties as needed.

This is the international version of Avoinkoodi.fi, which is intended for the Finnish speaking audience. This international version has two purposes: to potentially help foreign public sector officials as they may benefit from something we have already done in Finland, and to encourage creating something similar for other countries too.

Solita is one of the biggest vendors for digital business solutions for the Finnish public sector. The list of clients include Ministry of Justice, Finnish Transport Agency, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Finland Customs and others.
